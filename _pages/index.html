The French Quarter echoes with a chorus of street musicians, soulful melodies gracing the wrought-iron balconies of the colourful French townhouses. Southern hospitality abounds from down-to-earth eateries and eclectic dive-bars, where Creole flavours spice the gumbo, and forward-thinking chefs push the envelope on the city’s foodie offerings. High-society hotel bars offer timeless glamour alongside jazz bars that sing frenetic melodies deep into the night – New Orleans offers fortuitous adventure on every street.
